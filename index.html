About

Scott brings life into every conversation through his positive attitude and energy. He has spent over six years in retail management where he has honed his skills in leadership. He has grown in his knowledge in both HTML and CSS in order to meet the needs of his customers. He has also grown in his ability to maintain DNS Zone files and add new zone files in order to create custom subdodmains and email addresses for Appfolio customer's websites.
